#3 Arbitrage

This strategy attempts to buy and sell a commodity between different exchanges.

Commodities can be listed on multiple exchanges, often times these exchanges will have slight variations in pricing. A bot could query multiple exchanges to find out the difference in prices between them and buy on the cheaper exchange then sell on the more expensive one.

The picture on the right shows markets listing BTC/USDT pairs, notice that the current price of the pair on one is $13,536 and one is $13,557, that's a $21 difference the bot just has to buy on the cheaper market and sell on the more expensive one.

The challenge for this bot is that it has to check the fee amounts involved around transfering, buying, selling, etc, for each exchange. Dealing with multiple exchanges also increases the coding complexity.
