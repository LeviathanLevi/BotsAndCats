#2 Momentum

This strategy attempts to ride the waves of the market and buy/sell out when it detects a shift in momentum.

Momentum trading is often viewed as being thrown around by every push and pull of the market. A bot needs to react quickly and reflexively when the market signals a shift.

Say for example a commodity rises 1% in price, the bot could interpret this as as a momentum shift. The bot would buy in to ride the momentum up, once it buys in it can set a stop loss of say for example .5% and sell whenever the commodity drops .5% from it's peak.

Repeating this process cyclically could net a fair amount of profits if the commidity is volatile enough to swing up and down. Tuning the trailing stop order amount and buy in amounts is what will determine the success or failure of the bot.
