#5 Natural Language Processing

This strategy uses a bot to sweep the web and find articles/news then interprets the words and phrases used in it to decide on actionable trades.

One implementation would be to have the bot sweep the web for articles, tweets, news, etc. then interpret the words in the media to determine if the sentiment is negative or postive.

For example, if a bot was trading company X and it came across an article that said "Company Y acquires company X." then it could interpret this as a positive change and buy into the stock.

A bot has speed that can beat any human, the moment an article comes out a bot could scan and process the data on it in a matter of seconds. This means it can beat anyone to the punch on good news.

The biggest challenge is having the bot correctly determine if something is good news or bad news. It comes down to the underlying technology of the natural language processor.
